Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

🔒

RosterPayAU does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All information you enter stays entirely on your device.

📱

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

RosterPayAU is a fully offline application. All data you enter — including your name, wage, work hours, allowances, and payslip information — is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to any server.

☁️

2. Data Storage & Third Parties

We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising networks, or data collection services. We do not share any data with third parties — because no data ever leaves your device.

  • No analytics or tracking
  • No advertising SDKs
  • No cloud sync or backup by this app
  • No account creation required
📧

3. Email & Sharing Features

When you choose to email a payslip or share your roster, the app uses your device's built-in Mail or Messages app. RosterPayAU does not have access to your email account, contacts, or message history. Any sharing is entirely initiated and controlled by you.
